## Creating a Blog Post (5 minutes)

1. Go to `https://nidhielite.com/studio`
2. Click **Blog Post** â†’ **Create**
3. Fill in these fields:
   - **Title**: "Why Hosur is Perfect for Real Estate Investment"
   - **Slug**: Auto-fills (e.g., `why-hosur-perfect`)
   - **Author**: Select your name
   - **Main Image**: Click to upload a nice image
   - **Categories**: Select "Investment" or "Real Estate"
   - **Published Date**: Today
   - **Body**: Write your article (use formatting buttons for bold, lists, etc.)
   - **Excerpt**: "Discover why Hosur is becoming the next real estate hotspot..."

4. Click **Publish**
5. Your blog post appears at: `https://nidhielite.com/blog/why-hosur-perfect`

## Tips for Better SEO

When creating blog posts:
- Use keywords naturally in the title
- Write a compelling excerpt (150-160 characters)
- Add a featured image (1200x630px recommended)
- Use headings (H2, H3) to structure content
- Include internal links to your property pages
- Add relevant categories
